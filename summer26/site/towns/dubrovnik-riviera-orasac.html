Dubrovnik Riviera / Orašac

Best beauty-first Croatia base if you want a serious residence resort instead of a simple beach hotel.

This is the most photogenic foreign option in the pass. Orašac gives you sea views and resort calm, while Dubrovnik old town sits close enough for selective day trips instead of becoming the daily logistics burden.
