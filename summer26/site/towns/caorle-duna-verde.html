Town Report

Caorle / Duna Verde

The new high-upside family-luxury beach lane, especially because Cavallino Bianco opens here in 2026.

Caorle is farther than Jesolo but more interesting than a generic beach strip: family beaches, a pretty old town, cycling paths, and now a new luxury family hotel concept that could justify the extra transfer.

Quick take

Town fit for this trip

This page focuses on beach logistics, family rhythm, and what nearby map pins actually help with kids and grandparents.

Strengths

Why This Town Works

  • Families willing to trade a longer transfer for a purpose-built family resort or giant stay-put holiday centre.
  • Trips where the adults want a more charming coastal town nearby than pure resort-strip Jesolo.
Cautions

What to Watch

  • Cavallino Bianco is new in 2026, so it lacks mature guest reviews.
  • Pra delle Torri is huge and practical, but not luxury-hotel quiet.
Travel

Route Notes

  • Venezia Marco Polo and Treviso are both plausible by private transfer; neither is as easy as Jesolo.
  • Choose Caorle only if the property itself is the reason for the Italy plan.
