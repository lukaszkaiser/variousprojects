Grzybowo

Quiet beach base next to Kołobrzeg, with one of the easiest kitchenette answers.

Grzybowo is compelling because it gives you access to the Kołobrzeg region without forcing you to sleep inside the busier city. For small kids and grandparents, that can be a very useful compromise.
